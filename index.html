What NeuroPrime Is and Is Not

NeuroPrime is a dietary supplement designed to support focus and mental clarity for men over 40. It utilizes botanical extracts and amino acids intended to support cognitive function during demanding professional tasks, without relying on massive doses of raw stimulants.

It must be stated clearly: NeuroPrime is not a medical treatment. It is not formulated or approved to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any cognitive disease or severe neurodegenerative conditions like Alzheimer's. It is strictly nutritional support for a healthy baseline.

We avoid making strong medical promises; no supplement replaces fundamental health habits like adequate sleep and nutrition.

Who Should Avoid NeuroPrime

This supplement is formulated for a very specific adult demographic. You should definitively avoid purchasing NeuroPrime if you fall into any of the following categories:

  • You are under the age of 18.
  • You have a diagnosed neurodegenerative disease requiring supervised medical treatment.
  • You are pregnant or actively nursing.
  • You are currently prescribed blood thinners (botanicals like Ginkgo can increase risk of bleeding).
  • You are seeking an extreme, caffeinated "buzz" instead of sustained, subtle focus support.
